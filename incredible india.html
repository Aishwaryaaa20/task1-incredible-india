Incredible India

Taj Mahal, Agra

The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a symbol of eternal love.

  • Built in 1653
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Located on the banks of the Yamuna River

Hawa Mahal, Jaipur

Known as the Palace of Winds, this five-story pink sandstone structure has 953 windows to allow cool air inside. It was built in 1799 by Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh.

  • Famous for its unique honeycomb façade
  • Located in the Pink City of Jaipur
  • Designed for royal ladies to observe street festivities unseen

Kerala Backwaters

The backwaters of Kerala are a network of interconnected canals, rivers, lakes, and inlets. Known for serene houseboat cruises through lush greenery, it’s a peaceful escape.

  • Popular destinations: Alleppey, Kumarakom
  • Houseboat cruises offer traditional Kerala cuisine
  • Rich biodiversity with unique flora and fauna
